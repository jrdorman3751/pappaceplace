About us

A place where students feel at home.

Pappace Place Learning Center is a tutoring center in New Paltz, NY dedicated to helping elementary and middle school students build the skills and confidence they need to succeed — in school and beyond.

We believe every student learns differently. Our approach is patient, personalized, and always encouraging. Whether a student is catching up, keeping up, or getting ahead, we meet them exactly where they are.

🌱

Patient & Personalized

Every session is tailored to the individual student — their pace, their style, their goals.

💛

Warm & Encouraging

We create a safe space where students feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes.

🎯

Results Focused

We celebrate progress at every level — from small wins to big breakthroughs.
